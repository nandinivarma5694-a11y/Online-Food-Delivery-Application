Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

Browse our menu, select your favorite dishes, and click the "Order Now" button to proceed to checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, online wallets, and cash on delivery.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Yes, please contact our support team within 10 minutes of placing the order to make changes.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we deliver within a 10 km radius of our restaurant.
